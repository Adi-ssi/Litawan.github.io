Greek Yogurt Parfait

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Greek yogurt (plain, non-fat or full-fat)
  • 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup granola
  • 1/4 cup fresh berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl or glass, layer Greek yogurt, a drizzle of honey or maple syrup, granola, and berries.
  2. Repeat layers if you like and top with a few extra berries.
  3. Enjoy a sweet, creamy, and protein-packed treat.
