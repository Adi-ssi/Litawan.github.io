Cinnamon Sugar Dough Roll-Ups

Ingredients

  • 1 package refrigerated pizza dough or crescent roll dough
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Unroll the pizza dough or crescent roll dough onto a clean surface. If using pizza dough, roll it out into a rectangle.
  3. Brush the dough evenly with the melted butter.
  4. In a small bowl, mix the sugar and cinnamon together.
  5. Sprinkle the cinnamon-sugar mixture evenly over the buttered dough.
  6. Carefully roll the dough into a log, starting from one side to the other.
  7. Slice the dough into 1-inch pieces and place them on the prepared baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until golden brown and crispy.
  9. Let the cinnamon sugar dough roll-ups cool for a few minutes before serving.
