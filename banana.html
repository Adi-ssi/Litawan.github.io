Peanut Butter Banana Bites

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter (or almond butter)
  • A sprinkle of granola or chia seeds (optional)

Instructions

  1. Slice the banana into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Spread peanut butter on top of each banana slice.
  3. If desired, sprinkle with granola or chia seeds for extra crunch and texture.
  4. Enjoy these sweet, salty, and satisfying bites!
