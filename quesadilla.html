Mini Quesadillas

Ingredients

  • 2 small flour tortillas
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, or your favorite)
  • Salsa or guacamole for dipping (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat and place one tortilla in the pan.
  2. Sprinkle cheese over the tortilla and top with the second tortilla.
  3. Cook for about 2-3 minutes per side until golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  4. Slice into wedges and serve with salsa or guacamole.
