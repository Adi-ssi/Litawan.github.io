Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 8 oz spaghetti
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 slices bacon (chopped)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together eggs and Parmesan cheese.
  3. Cook the bacon in a skillet until crispy, then add garlic and saut√© for 1 minute.
  4. Toss the cooked spaghetti in the skillet with the bacon and garlic.
  5. Remove from heat, then quickly stir in the egg and cheese mixture, adding pasta water as needed for a creamy texture.
  6. Season with salt and pepper and serve immediately.
