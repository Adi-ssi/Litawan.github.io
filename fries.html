Sweet Potato Fries

Ingredients

  • 2 medium sweet potatoes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 tsp paprika or cinnamon (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Peel and cut the sweet potatoes into thin fries.
  3. Toss them with olive oil, salt, pepper, and paprika or cinnamon.
  4. Spread the fries in a single layer on a baking sheet.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes, flipping halfway through until crispy and golden.
  6. Enjoy with a dipping sauce like ketchup or a spicy mayo!
