Veggie Stir-Fry with Tofu

Ingredients

  • 1 block tofu (cubed)
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell peppers, carrots, snap peas, etc.)
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 garlic clove (minced)

Instructions

  1. Toss the tofu cubes with cornstarch and fry in vegetable oil until crispy. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same pan, heat sesame oil and saut√© garlic for 1 minute.
  3. Add the mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Stir in soy sauce and tofu, cooking for another 2 minutes.
  5. Serve over steamed rice or noodles.
