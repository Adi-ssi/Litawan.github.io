Simple Omelette Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 tbsp milk or water
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter or oil
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese (optional)
  • 1/4 cup chopped fillings (e.g., ham, onions, bell peppers, mushrooms, spinach, or tomatoes)

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl, add the milk or water, and season with salt and pepper. Whisk until well blended.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add the butter or oil. Swirl it around to coat the pan evenly.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet and tilt the pan so the eggs spread out evenly.
  4. Let the eggs cook undisturbed for 1-2 minutes until the edges start to set but the center is still slightly runny.
  5. Sprinkle the cheese and your chosen fillings evenly over one half of the omelette.
  6. Using a spatula, gently fold the other half over the fillings. Cook for another 1-2 minutes until the cheese melts and the omelette is fully set.
  7. Slide the omelette onto a plate, garnish as desired, and serve warm.
