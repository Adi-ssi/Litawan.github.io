Cheesy Garlic Breadsticks

Ingredients

  • Refrigerated pizza dough
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp dried Italian seasoning

Instructions

  1. Roll out the pizza dough and cut it into strips.
  2. Brush with melted butter, sprinkle with garlic powder, cheese, and Italian seasoning.
  3. Twist the strips if you want and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 10-12 minutes until golden brown.
  4. Serve with marinara sauce for dipping.
