A better cup noodle

Ingredients

  • Any kinds of cup noodle
  • Eggs
  • Any kinds of protein

Instructions

  1. Cook the Noodles: Start by preparing your cup noodles according to the package instructions. Boil water, pour it into the cup with the noodles, and let it cook for the recommended time, usually 3-4 minutes. Stir occasionally to ensure the noodles cook evenly.
  2. Eat the Noodles: Once the noodles are cooked, enjoy eating them as usual, leaving behind the soup (broth) in the cup.
  3. Prepare the Egg Soup: After finishing the noodles, take one or two eggs (depending on your preference) and crack them into a separate small bowl.
  4. Microwave the Soup: Pour the leftover soup from the cup noodles into a microwave-safe bowl. You can also add a bit of water if you want to stretch the broth or adjust the flavor.
  5. Add the Eggs: Gently beat the eggs in the bowl and slowly pour them into the hot leftover soup. Stir the soup slightly to break the eggs into smaller pieces or leave them in larger portions, depending on how you like your egg soup.
  6. Microwave the Egg Soup: Microwave the bowl of egg soup for 1-2 minutes. Stir every 30 seconds to ensure the eggs cook evenly and do not overcook.
  7. Add Protein (Optional): You can add cooked protein, such as shredded chicken, ground beef, tofu, or even leftover meat, to the soup after microwaving. Simply stir it in to warm it through or allow it to heat up in the microwave for another 30 seconds.
  8. Serve and Enjoy: Once the eggs are fully cooked and the protein is heated, your egg soup is ready to serve! Pour the soup into your cup or bowl, and enjoy the added flavors and protein boost.
